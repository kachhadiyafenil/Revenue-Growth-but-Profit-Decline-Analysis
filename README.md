# 📊 Revenue Growth but Profit Decline Analysis (Power BI)

## 🔍 Problem Statement

The company is experiencing an increase in revenue, but profit is not growing at the same rate. This indicates potential issues in pricing strategy, discounting, or product performance. The objective of this project is to identify the root causes impacting profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze revenue vs profit trends over time
* Identify loss-making categories and products
* Investigate the impact of discounts and returns
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Sample Superstore Dataset (Kaggle)
* Tables Used:

  * Orders
  * Returns
  * People

---

## 🧠 Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin (%)
* Profit per Order
* Return Rate (%)
* Average Discount (%)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Sections

### 🟢 1. Overview

* Revenue vs Profit Trend
* KPI Cards (Revenue, Profit, Margin, Profit per Order)

📌 Insight:
Revenue is increasing steadily, but profit is fluctuating and not growing proportionally. The gap between revenue and profit is widening.

---

### 🟡 2. Problem Area Analysis

* Profit by Category
* Profit by Sub-Category

📌 Insight:
Furniture is the least profitable category. Sub-categories like Tables and Bookcases are generating negative profit.

---

### 🔴 3. Root Cause Analysis

* Discount % by Sub-Category
* Return Rate % by Sub-Category

📌 Insight:

* Tables (26% discount, ~9.7% return rate)
* Bookcases (21% discount, ~6.7% return rate)

High discounting is the primary driver of low profit margins, while high return rates further contribute to losses.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategy (avoid excessive discounting)
* Review pricing for loss-making products
* Investigate high return rates (product quality or expectations)
* Focus on high-margin product categories

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling

---

## 🚀 Key Learnings

* Importance of data modeling and relationships
* Using DAX to create business KPIs
* Building dashboards with storytelling approach
* Performing root cause analysis using data
